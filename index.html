Difference between Padding,Margin and Border

CSS uses what's called the box model to tell web browsers how to display and space different elements on a web page. All websites are made up of a series of boxes and these boxes have four major components.

Content

The innermost part of the box. Content could be an image, text, heading, or something else.

Padding

The inner space between the content and the border of your box.

Border

The perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line like the green one pictured above.

Margin

The outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.
